    After You Apply

    • Your ID will generally be mailed within 2 weeks if you meet the requirements
    • The Municipal ID cannot be used to vote in Wisconsin
    • The ID expires after 5 years
    • Notify the License Division within 10 days if your address changes or your ID is lost or stolen
    • If you move outside the City of Milwaukee, the ID will no longer be valid
